Directions

If you're traveling by plane, your nearest airport is City Airport Bremen. Take Line 6 of the tram service, operated by BSAG, Bremer Straßenbahn AG, the local tram and bus service. The tram station is right outside the airport entrance and goes directly from Bremen Airport (Bremen Flughafen) to Bremen University (Universität).

Take tram Line 6 to Central Station (Hauptbahnhof) if you're traveling by train. Purchase tickets at the airport (exit), Central Station, or on the trams at the automatic ticket machines operated by BSAG. Buy a "7 Days Ticket" for 25.40 EUR. It's valid in Bremen-City (Zone 1) for seven consecutive days (no NightLine surcharge included).

The venue of the workshop is located directly at the A27 if you're traveling by car.

For information on visa regulations, please visit the Federal Foreign Office's webpages.
